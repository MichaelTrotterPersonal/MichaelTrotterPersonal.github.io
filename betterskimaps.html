Better Ski Area Maps

Have you ever gone to a ski resort you weren't familiar with and sat staring at the list of lift statuses and the trail map side-by-side trying to figure out which areas were open and which weren't?

When Mick moved to Canberra in 2017 he found himself frustrated that Perisher didn't display their lift status reports on top of their trail map, so he wrote a Python script to take care of that. The web app scrapes the Perisher lift status page and colour-codes each status onto their trail map.
